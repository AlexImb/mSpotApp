Your meetings are easier than ever

Have you ever wanted to meet with your friends or your beloved ones, but one of you got lost on the road, due to the big distance, haven’t reached the meeting location in time, or you couldn’t simply decide the place of your meeting? Now you can share wonderful moments with your friends without any problems, and all you have to do is to open Mspot and sign up with Facebook.

Your friends are at a tap of finger away.

You can create meetings and join your friends in theirs, with a code generated by the app or by simply adding them from your friends list, which consists in your facebook friends who have the app on their phones. Once you created the meeting, you will receive a code that can be sent to your friends. They will join the session by entering the received code into the special box, in the app or by accepting your invitation. Find your friends and start meetings.

Choose the place of sharing
laughter and love.

You decided to share real smiles with your friends, you have created the meeting and the map was opened. All you have to do now is to choose the best location. The app can calculate the distance between you and all your friends and is able to indicate the most suitable location regarding the distance between you. Still undecided? You can tap words like „coffe” or „cinema” in the search section and you’ll receive some suggestions for locations to choose. Now, the place of meeting is not a problem anymore.

Meetings beyond a single spot
on the map.

You want to go with your friends at an outdoor concert to feel the vibe of the music in your chest, or you want to see each other in park, without establishing a precised spot of meeting? Now you can choose an area of meeting instead of a spot on the map. The area will be set around a dot on the map and you can resize it however you want. It can be the size of a park or even a city.

Now it’s time to move on
and see your beloved ones.

Now that you set the meeting details, it’s time to go to your friends. The app can suggest the best roads so you can reach at your destination in time, by buss, by car, by bike or by walking. You can also be able to see what route chose one of your friends, the transport they preffered or where they are.

Keep in touch with your friends
on your road.

Don’t lose the bound between you and your friends for any minute. You can communicate with them live while you’re alone on the road and everything will feel better with a friend in touch. Share ideas, wishes, talk about what’s on your mind or about what you see around you and get live notifications from them.

A whole world at your wrist.

Everything gets simplier than you thought. Your travel experience can get better than ever because you have all the informations you need on your wrist. Just the simple movement of your hand, a finger tap or some few words cand be enough to get all the data you need when you travel. Notifications, maps, locations and live messages with your friends. All of this on your watch, thing wich makes it easier to get your informations when you drive, when you’re in subway or bus, or when you’re simply in hurry. Take navigation to the next level.
